Below we have our top rated Romance books

THE BOOK IN FIFTH PLACE is The Tea Planter’s Wife by Dinah Jefferies. A wonderful story about a young women who travels to Ceylon to join her husband on his tea plantation but there is more than meets the eye and a lot of secrets. A beautifully written story that captures the reader.

THE BOOK IN FOURTH PLACE is Eleanor & Park by Rainbow Rowell. A heart-warming story that follows two characters who couldn’t be more different that fall for each other. A young adult/teen romance book with some very special characters.

THE BOOK IN THIRD PLACE is Me Before You by Jojo Moyes. A story that follows an ordinary girl who takes on a new job that changes her life. She meets Will Traynor who is wheelchair bound after an accident. A beautiful love story with a captivating plot that will break your heart.

THE BOOK IN SECOND PLACE is The Notebook by Nicholas Sparks . A beautiful story set in North Carolina that follows Noah and Ally. Two people who met many years ago and spent a beautiful summer together and just couldn’t let each other go.

THE BOOK IN FIRST PLACE is Pride And Prejudice by Jane Austen. One of the most popular English novels, this romantic story follows Elizabeth Bennet and her lover Mr Darcy. A descriptive story and brilliantly written story.

Our top five Romance books
  • #5 The Tea Planters Wife by Dinah Jefferies
  • #4 Eleanor & Park by Rainbow Rowell
  • #3 Me Before You by Jojo Moyes
  • #2 The Notebook by Nicholas Sparks
  • #1 Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen
