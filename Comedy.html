(WHO THIS GENRE IS FOR) (THIS IS JUST A PLACEHOLDER DESCRIPTION). This genre is includes books about a series of funny or comical events that are intended to make the reader laugh. Everyone enjoys a good laugh and these books are the best place to start.

Below we have our top rated Comedy books

THE BOOK IN FIFTH PLACE is At Freddie's by Penelope Fitzgerald, it is a tragicomedy that is set in a London stage school in the early 1960s that draws directly on her personal experiences.

THE BOOK IN FOURTH PLACE is Based on a True Story by Norm Macdonald, written by one of the greatest stand-up comics of all time, this book is a a manuscript about comedy, tragedy, love, loss, war, and redemption.

THE BOOK IN THIRD PLACE is Bossypants by Tina Fey, it is a memoir which recounts episodes from her life along with her career as a comedian and writer for Saturday Night Live and 30 Rock.

THE BOOK IN SECOND PLACE is Me Talk Pretty One Day by David Sedaris, it is a collection of twenty-eight short, hilarious essays, he writes about all his experiences and teaches us that knowledge can sometimes come from the unlikeliest of places.

THE BOOK IN FIRST PLACE and our personal favourite is Everyone Hanging Out Without Me? (And Other Concerns) by Mindy Kaling, it includes witty confessions and unscientific observations as she writes about everything from being a timid young girl to living the Hollywood life.
