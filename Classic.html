(WHO THIS GENRE IS FOR) A classic is a book is a book that is deemed as being exemplary or noteworthy and makes a contribution to literature. There are all kinds of classics, from horror to romance to a novel that changed how a genre was written.

Below we have our top rated Classic books

THE BOOK IN FIFTH PLACE is The Handmaid's Tale by Margaret Atwood, it is a dystopian novel set in New England that follows a Christian fundamentalist regime in the former United States that arose as a response to a fertility crisis.

THE BOOK IN FOURTH PLACE is Little Women by Louisa May Alcott, it is an autobiographical account of her life and her three sisters. As their father is fighting in the American Civil War, the sisters are at home with their mother, a very outspoken women for her time.

THE BOOK IN THIRD PLACE is A Tale of Two Cities by Charles Dickens, it follows the story of the French Doctor Manette during the French Revolution, his 18-year-long imprisonment in the Bastille in Paris and his release to live in London with his daughter Lucie, whom he had never met.

THE BOOK IN SECOND PLACE is To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee, it follows the story of Scout Finch, her brother and their father during the Great Depression. Their father, Atticus, is a lawyer and their family is reasonably well off in comparison to the rest of society.

THE BOOK IN FIRST PLACE and our personal favourite is Anna Karenina by Leo Tolstoy, it is a tragic story that follows the rise and fall of the love affair between Anna Karenina and Count Vronsky and the conflict between human desires and societal roles.

Our top five Classic books
  • #5 The Handmaid's Tale by Margaret Atwood
  • #4 Little Women by Louisa May Alcott
  • #3 A Tale of Two Cities by Charles Dickens
  • #2 To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee
  • #1 Anna Karenina by Leo Tolstoy
