Book Sorter

Our organiation values

We are an organisation that actively works to promote books that display diversity. We feel as though it is important that everyone can recognise themselves in litrature and learn about others cultures and experiences. Reading is an amazing way to explore the world and Book Sorter has different lists of our favourite books that are sorted into their genres. If you dont know where to start, take a look at our explore page or if you know just what you're looking for then use the dropdown menu to select your genre.
