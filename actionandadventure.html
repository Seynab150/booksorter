Action and Adventure

...

(WHO THIS GENRE IS FOR) Action and Adventure books is the literary genre that includes spy novels, adventure stories, tales of terror, intrigue and mysteries. These books provide a fast paced plot to follow where the charcters will usually have to face some sort of danger.

Below we have our top rated Action and Adventure books

THE BOOK IN FIFTH PLACE is Kidnapped by Robert Louis Stevenson, it tells the story of David Balfour, a young man whos is recently orphan. As David has now turned seventeen years old, he has decided it is time to go and seek his fortune.

THE BOOK IN FOURTH PLACE is The Lightning Thief by Rick Riordan, it follows the story of Percy Jackson as he undertakes a quest to journey across the United States to catch a thief who has stolen Zeus' master bolt; the original weapon of mass destruction.

THE BOOK IN THIRD PLACE is The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown, it follows the story of Robert Langdon and Sophie Neveu after a murder in the Louvre Museum leads to their involvment in a battle between the Priory of Sion and Opus Dei.

THE BOOK IN SECOND PLACE is Moby Dick by Herman Melville, it tells the story of a voyage across the world on the whaling ship the Pequod and its captain, Ahab, as they pursues the great Sperm Whale; Moby Dick.

THE BOOK IN FIRST PLACE and our personal favourite is Treasure Island by Robert Louis Stevenson, it tells the story of young boy called Jim, as he watched over an inn in an English seaside town. When a new guest arrives and at the inn and scares everyone with threats of violence.

Our top five Action and Adventure books
  • #5 Kidnapped by Robert Louis Stevenson
  • #4 The Lightning Thief by Rick Riordan
  • #3 The Da Vinci Code by Dan Brown
  • #2 Moby Dick by Herman Melville
  • #1 Treasure Island by Robert Louis Stevenson
